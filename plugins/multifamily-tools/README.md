# Multifamily Tools Plugin

A two-skill acquisition toolkit for multifamily investors — from a broker OM landing in your inbox to a Go/No-Go verdict before releasing contingencies on apartments, duplexes, and larger residential income properties.

## What's New in v1.0

- **Client preferences saved to memory** — your buy box (asset class, target markets, cap rate, min DSCR, min cash flow, unit count range, financing approach) is captured once and applied to every analysis automatically
- **Screener now saves to memory** — the Phase 0 screener output is stored so the underwriter picks up where it left off
- **All thresholds calibrated to your criteria** — offer scenarios, viability scores, pursuit decisions, and Go/No-Go verdicts reference your stated minimums

## Skills

### Multifamily Screener (Phase 0 — Pre-LOI)

Fast deal analysis through the eyes of a skeptical lead analyst. Drop in a broker OM, T12, rent roll, or a verbal summary and get:

- Client preferences check (reads from memory or runs a 60-second setup)
- Rent roll reality check — physical vacancy vs. stated, current vs. market rents
- Conservative NOI with mandatory expense normalizations (including post-sale tax reassessment)
- 9-cell valuation matrix calibrated to your target cap rate and asset class
- Creative finance stack (assumption + seller carry + MLO + PML gap)
- True cash flow vs. your stated minimums
- 3 offer scenarios with your preferred financing approach as Offer 1
- Viability score (1–10) with buy box fit check
- Red flag audit including submarket risks, deferred maintenance signals, income manipulation flags
- Complete draft LOI ready to send
- Saves screener summary to memory for the underwriter

**Trigger phrases:** "screen this apartment deal," "analyze this multifamily OM," "what should I offer on this complex," "quick look at this rent roll," "run the numbers on this apartment building," "should I send an LOI"

---

### Multifamily Underwriter (Phase 1 — Post-Contract)

Comprehensive due diligence for apartment deals under contract. Reads your preferences AND the screener's saved analysis automatically, then verifies everything before you go hard on contingencies.

- Loads client preferences + screener findings from memory
- 6-step revenue verification: bank statements, Schedule E cross-check, physical vacancy, rent roll audit, other income verification, year-over-year trend
- Full expense normalization including post-sale property tax reassessment
- Three-scenario NOI (Floor / Base / Upside)
- Full valuation matrix with client's target cap rate highlighted
- DSCR at contract, recommended, and floor prices vs. your minimum
- Risk Matrix across 4 categories (Financial, Legal/Regulatory, Physical/Infrastructure, Market)
- 30-item prioritized due diligence checklist
- 25 hard seller questions tailored to the deal
- Gated Go/No-Go verdict (GREEN / YELLOW / RED) calibrated to your risk tolerance

**Trigger phrases:** "underwrite this apartment deal," "run full multifamily due diligence," "verify this deal," "go deep on this property," "we're under contract analyze everything," "multifamily DD"

---

## Two-Phase System

**Phase 0 — Screener** → Fast analysis, draft LOI, saves deal to memory.
**Phase 1 — Underwriter** → Reads screener output, verifies all figures, delivers verdict before going hard.

## Updating Your Preferences

Run either skill and say "update my preferences" at any time. Your current settings display and you can change any field. Changes save to memory immediately.
